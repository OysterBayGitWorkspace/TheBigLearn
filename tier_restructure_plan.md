# VC Dojo — 5-Tier Restructuring Plan

## The Problem

Right now everything lives in 3 tiers where Tier 1 mixes "What is product-market fit?" with "What is a ROFR?" — two completely different knowledge levels. An investment team member shouldn't have to wade through 179 general definitions to prove they know what a pitch deck is before they can learn about liquidation waterfalls.

## The New Structure

| Tier | Name | Purpose | Skippable? | Unlock Rule |
|------|------|---------|------------|-------------|
| 1 | **Basics** | General VC ecosystem knowledge — what ops teams need | YES — can skip to Tier 2 | Always open |
| 2 | **Term Sheet Essentials** | Every definition you'll encounter in a real term sheet | No | Open by default (or complete 70% of Basics) |
| 3 | **Differentiate & Compare** | "What's the difference between X and Y?" — pattern recognition | No | 70% of Tier 2 |
| 4 | **Applied Scenarios** | Calculations, real-world applications, negotiation judgment | No | 70% of Tier 3 |
| 5 | **Expert Scenarios** | Multi-concept German-specific edge cases, pressure situations | No | 70% of Tier 4 |

### Key Design Decision: Tier 1 is Skippable

Tier 2 ("Term Sheet Essentials") should unlock by default OR after 70% of Tier 1. This means:
- Investment team members can jump straight to Tier 2
- Ops/marketing team can start from Tier 1 to build context
- Nobody is forced to prove they know what "burn rate" means
